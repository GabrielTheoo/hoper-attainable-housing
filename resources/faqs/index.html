About Hoper

About Hoper

Hoper is a housing innovation company and a division of Attainable Housing Advocates, a group focused on expanding access to sustainable homeownership. Through Hoper, eligible participants can earn income at closing while contributing to research focused on improving housing affordability.
No. Hoper is not a mortgage lender. We work alongside lenders, real estate professionals, and homebuyers.
No. Hoper is a social innovation company and a division of the Attainable Housing Advocates Group.
A research initiative that allows eligible participants to earn income when purchasing a home. Participants complete structured financial education and may earn up to 3.5% of the home's purchase price — up to $13,000 — at closing.
