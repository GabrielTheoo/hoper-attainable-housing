For Home Builders

Helping Builders Work With Financially Stronger Buyers

Homebuilders play a critical role in shaping the future of affordable homeownership. Hoper partners with builders who care about long-term homeowner success, energy-efficient housing, and sustainable financial outcomes for the families they serve. Through the Hoper program, we help strengthen the financial position of qualified buyers, creating more confidence at closing and greater stability after move-in.

Partner With Hoper

A Smarter Housing Affordability Solution

Many buyers technically qualify for a mortgage but enter homeownership with limited financial reserves and uncertainty about long-term housing costs. Hoper strengthens buyer readiness through:

Pre-closing homebuyer education
Earned income through research participation
Post-closing financial mentorship
Fully owned solar integrated through FHA provisions

Eligible participants may earn up to 3.5% of the purchase price (up to $13,000) — helping buyers enter your homes with stronger reserves and greater financial stability.

Why Builders Partner With Hoper

Builders who collaborate with Hoper gain more than an added feature. They gain a structured way to strengthen buyer readiness and differentiate their communities.

Greater buyer confidence in energy-efficient housing
Clear education around the long-term value of solar ownership
Buyers who arrive at closing with stronger financial positioning
Differentiation in competitive housing markets

Because the Hoper program incorporates fully owned solar, builders offering energy-efficient homes can present a clear, long-term value proposition to buyers.

Education That Reduces Buyer Uncertainty

The Hoper program includes a structured homebuyer education course that prepares buyers to understand:

Their mortgage structure
Long-term housing costs
Energy savings through solar and financial planning beyond closing

When buyers understand what they are purchasing and why it matters, decisions move forward with greater clarity and less hesitation.
