Give Your FHA Borrowers a Stronger Start

As a loan officer, you guide families into homeownership. Hoper gives you a structured way to strengthen that outcome before and after closing. Through the Hoper program, eligible borrowers can earn up to 3.5% of the purchase price (up to $13,000) while completing homeownership education and committing to post-closing mentorship. Underwriting Ready

Built to Support Loan Performance

Hoper was designed with underwriting realities in mind. Participants earn documented 1099 income before closing. Funds are sourced and verified. Solar is incorporated through existing FHA solar provisions. There are no second liens and no rate increases. We recommend borrowers meet their minimum required investment with their own assets and use program earnings conservatively within FHA interested party contribution limits. The structure is intentional, compliant, and aligned with lender best practices.

Strengthen Credit Readiness and Long-Term Stability

Many FHA borrowers qualify, but enter ownership with limited reserves and high debt-to-income ratios. Hoper addresses that gap. Borrowers complete pre-closing home buyer education focused on budgeting, credit readiness, and understanding long-term housing costs. After closing, they continue with financial mentorship centered on emergency reserves, debt reduction, and long-term planning.

Pre-Closing Education

Self-paced online curriculum covering budgeting, credit, and mortgage structure.

Emergency Reserve Building

Post-closing mentorship focused on building financial cushion after move-in.

Debt Reduction Strategy

Structured guidance on reducing debt and improving long-term financial health.

Long-Term Planning

Ongoing mentorship that keeps borrowers on track well beyond closing day.

Energy-Efficient Housing Without Separate Solar Debt

Under FHA solar provisions, borrowers can incorporate fully owned solar into their mortgage structure rather than rely on high-cost, standalone solar loans. While the loan amount adjusts, monthly utility expenses decrease — improving the predictability of total housing costs. For borrowers concerned about rising energy costs, this becomes a practical conversation — one that aligns credit costs with long-term, energy-efficient housing.

Simple to Offer and Easy to Implement

Referring a borrower to Hoper takes less than a minute. Once connected, our team handles enrollment, documentation, income verification, and solar coordination. You receive the necessary documentation for your file, including verified pay stubs, depository statements, and the solar contract.
