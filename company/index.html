About Us

We Exist to Make Homeownership Attainable for Every Family

Attainable House Advocates was founded on a simple belief: the path to owning a home shouldn't be blocked by confusion, cost, or a lack of the right connections.

Our Mission

Closing the Gap Between Aspiration and Ownership

Millions of American families want to own a home but don't know where to start, don't think they qualify, or have been turned away without a clear path forward. We built Hoper to change that.

Through a combination of honest education, expert advocacy, and a network of trusted professionals, we help buyers understand their real position, identify every dollar of assistance available to them, and move confidently toward closing day.

We've helped buyers in most U.S. states — many of whom were told they "weren't ready" — become homeowners. Our average buyer unlocks over $13,000 in incentives and savings they didn't know existed.

Education

We demystify the homebuying process with plain-language guides, eligibility tools, and a resource library built for real buyers — not financial professionals.

Advocacy

We advocate for our buyers at every step — connecting them with professionals who prioritize their interests, and fighting to remove barriers that stand between families and homeownership.

Research

We continuously research available grants, down payment programs, and incentives so our buyers always have access to the latest and most valuable opportunities in their area.

Our Values

What We Believe

Homeownership is a right, not a privilege

Every family deserves the stability, wealth-building potential, and sense of belonging that comes from owning a home. We exist to remove barriers, not reinforce them.

Transparency builds trust

We tell buyers exactly where they stand, what they qualify for, and what needs to improve — even when that conversation is hard. No false hope, no hidden fees, no surprises.

Education is the most powerful tool

An informed buyer makes better decisions, closes with more confidence, and builds lasting financial health. We invest heavily in making complex information simple and accessible.

Solar and clean energy are homebuyer advantages

When new homes come with owned solar panels, buyers gain an immediate energy savings benefit and a significant tax credit. We help buyers understand and capture these advantages from day one.
